Build a mobile app:

Supported for all kinds of iOS and Android phone versions

The app is a trading card CRM that allows collectors and vendors manage their collectible items like cards and sealed products via a kanban interface with following stages:

- Acquired - add items that were acquired here with ability to add picture, price, condition (for graded cards their grading company and grade)
- In Transit - means the item is being shipped to collector or to grading company
- Grading - being graded by grading company with estimated timeline of arrival to collector
- In Stock - items in stock
- Listed - visible on the public store/collection page
- Sold - sold, collectors can choose which channel they have sold it through and choose the price and channel to show in analytics dashboard (cost of acquisition, channel, final price)

When collector/vendor has added their inventory they can publish the listed column into their public shop/collection page that they can share via a personal URL in any marketplace or group

This APP has to have database for One Piece, including all sealed products, cards with images and descriptions, their market price (we have a pricing database we can integrate later for actual pricing)

In collectors account, create the following fields:

- Username (Shop or Collector name)
- Handle (how their shop will be displayed in a link)
- Location
- Types of payment accepted (Paypal G&S, Venmo, Zelle, Cashapp, Cash, Crypto, other)
- Fulfillment options (shipping, meetups locaclly, meetups travel)
- Tradeshows user will be at (ability to add multiple with links)
- Wishlist (what user wants to buy, a textbox)
- References (ability to add multiple reference sources via links)

We have a prototype with colors and design for browser/desktop for the reference I'm attaching screenshots. Do a research on how top CRMs visualize their kanban or list for deals/items and adopt best practices for mobile
