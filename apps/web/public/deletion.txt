DATA DELETION INSTRUCTIONS

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

If you wish to delete your Slabhub account and associated data, please follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account at https://slabhub.gg/login
2. Navigate to the "Settings" page (https://slabhub.gg/settings)
3. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section.
4. Click on the "Delete Account" button.
5. Confirm your decision in the popup dialog.

Alternatively, you can request data deletion by contacting us via email:

Email: privacy@slabhub.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Upon receiving your request via email, we will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days.

Note on Data Retention:
When you delete your account, your personal information (name, email, etc.) will be permanently erased or anonymized. Some non-personal data may be retained for analytical purposes or as required by law.
